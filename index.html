ABOUT PROJECT

An elite building in the city center

An elite building in the city center that brings together people with high demands on living space and common interests. The main task of this project was to create a landing page for a luxury condominium in Kyiv, that would introduce users to the best aspects of living in this complex.

SOLUTIONS
  • Instead of presenting too much content at once, we divided it into meaningful sections and microinteractions
  • Created a smooth content flow to keep users engaged and oriented
  • The structure is clear and easy to follow, allowing users to effortlessly explore key sections
  • From browsing floor plans to contacting the sales department
